❖ About Us ❖

How We Work

Welcome to WEEKENDS, your gateway to unforgettable journeys. Founded by passionate travelers in 1980, we specialize in crafting personalized adventures that leave you with cherished memories.

Our Promise
At WEEKENDS, we're dedicated to providing:

Unique Experiences: We believe in the magic of discovery. Our handpicked destinations and itineraries offer a fresh perspective on the world.

Local Insights: Our expert guides are locals who bring their culture and stories to life, giving you an authentic travel experience.

Seamless Journeys: From the moment you book, we ensure that every step of your journey is seamless, leaving you free to enjoy your adventure without worry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create enriching travel experiences that broaden minds, bring cultures together, and promote sustainability through responsible tourism. We aim to inspire our travelers to explore the world with curiosity and respect for local cultures.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the world's leading provider of personalized travel experiences, setting the standard for excellence in the travel industry. We strive to lead the way in offering sustainable travel options that contribute to the well-being of the environment and communities we visit.
